UX Design and Research Masterclass

Certificate: Yes
Cost: ₦70000
Program Length: 4 weeks
Start date: June 28 2025

About the course

This document outlines a 4-week UX Design and Research Masterclass designed to equip participants with both foundational knowledge and practical skills in key areas of user experience design, including human-centered design, design thinking, and UX research. The masterclass is fully instructor-led, with each module facilitated by a different subject-matter expert to ensure diverse perspectives and deep expertise. To reinforce learning, hands-on workshops will follow each module, and participants will engage in real-life project implementations that simulate industry scenarios, enhancing their problem-solving and product development capabilities.

What you will learn

  • Understand key differences between UX and UI
  • Learn and apply the principles of Human-Centered Design
  • Gain deep insights into the Design Thinking framework
  • Develop and execute a UX Research Plan
  • Build user personas, journey maps, and prototypes
  • Practice hands-on learning through weekly workshops
  • Work on a real-life project that applies concepts learned
  • Receive mentorship from 4 experienced instructors
  • Earn a Certificate of Participation

Delivery Model

Live In-Person Sessions every Saturday
Project submissions will be reviewed by instructors and peers
Each session is 4 hours, with lectures, breakout activities, and workshop time
Participants will receive materials, resources, and assignments for the week

Who is this for?

Aspiring UX Designers

Individuals who are new to the field and eager to build a strong foundation in UX design principles, human-centered design, and UX research. This program is ideal for those looking to launch a career in UX.

Product Designers and Managers

Professionals involved in product development need a better grasp of user experience to enhance product usability and customer satisfaction. The curriculum will empower them to lead user-centered design initiatives within their teams.

Tech Professionals Transitioning to UX:

Software developers, digital marketers, and other tech professionals are interested in shifting their focus towards UX design. This masterclass provides a step-by-step guide from foundational concepts to practical application.

UX Researchers

Professionals interested in or currently practicing UX research who wish to sharpen their skills in planning and conducting research, including user interviews, surveys, and usability tests. This masterclass provides a comprehensive overview of integrating research into the design process

Location: Webinar

About the course

Developing a unique brand voice, building a strong online presence, insights on content monetization in Rwanda

What you will learn

  • Understand the importance of personal branding in the digital age.
  • Develop a unique content style and brand voice.
  • Build a compelling online presence.
  • Gain insights into monetizing content in Rwanda.

Who is this for?

Rwandan Creatives & Influencers (Emerging to Mid-Level)

Young Rwandans active on social media (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X), musicians, designers, photographers, bloggers, etc. Stand out with a distinct brand, grow a loyal audience, and start earning from their content.

Entrepreneurial Youth & Personal Brands

Students, freelancers, and solo entrepreneurs ready to shape a strong personal brand, attract clients, and use content as a pipeline for income and opportunity. This program helps them show up confidently online with storytelling that converts.

Rwandan Startups & Small Businesses

Founders and small teams selling fashion, food, beauty, or tech products/services who want to master content creation, connect with younger audiences, and use social platforms to grow their business and brand identity.

Diaspora Creatives Targeting Rwandan Audiences

Creatives abroad who want to stay connected with or build influence in Rwanda.They’ll learn how to craft content that resonates in Rwanda, understand the local monetization landscape, and sharpen their online presence from anywhere.
